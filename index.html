Hardware Repair

Hardware repair involves diagnosing and fixing physical computer components. Laptop motherboards are compact and mostly soldered, making repairs delicate, while desktop motherboards are modular and easier to upgrade. Key components include the CPU, RAM, storage, power connectors, expansion slots, and cooling system. Effective repair requires proper inspection, testing, and thermal management.
